Tense Formulas

These are the essential formulas for English tenses, designed to help you construct sentences clearly and correctly.

Present Tenses

Simple Present
Subject + base verb (s/es)
She eats lunch.
Present Continuous
Subject + is/am/are + verb-ing
They are studying.
Present Perfect
Subject + has/have + past participle
I have finished my work.
Present Perfect Continuous
Subject + has/have been + verb-ing
We have been waiting for an hour.

Past Tenses

Simple Past
Subject + past verb
He played soccer.
Past Continuous
Subject + was/were + verb-ing
I was watching TV.
Past Perfect
Subject + had + past participle
She had left before I arrived.
Past Perfect Continuous
Subject + had been + verb-ing
They had been working all day.

Future Tenses

Simple Future
Subject + will + base verb
We will travel tomorrow.
Future Continuous
Subject + will be + verb-ing
He will be sleeping at 10 PM.
Future Perfect
Subject + will have + past participle
I will have finished the task.
Future Perfect Continuous
Subject + will have been + verb-ing
She will have been working for 5 hours.
